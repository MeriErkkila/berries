Information about project

We have project assignment for spring 2023 “Visualization of berry data”. Goal of our project was creating a website that promotes sustainable development (SDG) values and demonstrate outcomes of data. The project utilizes data from the Natural Resources Center and open data from elsewhere (e.g. Finnish Meteorological Institute).

Our group decided to focus on the correlation of location of the berries to their growth. We also connected the berry growth to sustainable development goals, focusing on goals 2, 11 and 15. Findings of our research can be found on the other pages. Our team consists of five people, among who we divided our work. Our project manager, Emiliia Zemskova, focused on creating the introduction video for us. Website for the project was created by Meri Erkkilä and Ivan Sychevskiy using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Miklós Rézmüves and Ivan Khomyanin worked with the data and found the correlations. More info about our team members and roles can be found from the video and page "about us".

The project outcomes will be presented on project exhibition on April 27, 2023
