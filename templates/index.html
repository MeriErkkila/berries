What are we doing

We have project assignment for spring 2023 “Visualization of berry data”

Goal of our project is creating a website that promotes sustainable development (SDG) values and demonstrate outcomes of data. The project utilizes data from the Natural Resources Center and open data (e.g. Finnish Meteorological Institute) The project outcomes will be presented on project exhibition on April 27, 2023
